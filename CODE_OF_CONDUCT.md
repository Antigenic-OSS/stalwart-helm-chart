# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

Expected behavior:
- Be respectful in language and technical disagreement.
- Focus discussion on code, design, and evidence.
- Accept feedback and correct mistakes quickly.

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or bad-faith argumentation.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participants for violations.
